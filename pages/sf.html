ScavengerFun

Image recognition scavenger hunt played using a cell phone's camera

ScanvergerFun

This was a project from WildHacks, the Hackathon at Northwestern University. It was completed in under 36 Hrs. The explanation is quite simple: it's a scavenger hunt that uses convolutional neural nets (via API) to recognize objects in images.
